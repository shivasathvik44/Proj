IELTS Exam - 20 MCQs

1. She _____ to the gym every morning.

Correct Answer: goes

2. The synonym of “rapid” is:

Correct Answer: fast

3. Choose the correct spelling:

Correct Answer: Accommodation

4. If I _____ more time, I would travel around the world.

Correct Answer: had

5. The antonym of “scarce” is:

Correct Answer: abundant

6. Which sentence is correct?

Correct Answer: She doesn’t like coffee.

7. The word “benevolent” means:

Correct Answer: kind

8. Choose the correct preposition: “She is good _____ mathematics.”

Correct Answer: at

9. The plural of “crisis” is:

Correct Answer: crises

10. He _____ here since 2010.

Correct Answer: has lived

11. The synonym of “transparent” is:

Correct Answer: clear

12. Choose the correct article: “She bought _____ umbrella.”

Correct Answer: an

13. The past tense of “teach” is:

Correct Answer: taught

14. Which sentence is correct?

Correct Answer: They have a car.

15. The antonym of “ancient” is:

Correct Answer: modern

16. He is taller _____ his brother.

Correct Answer: than

17. The opposite of “expand” is:

Correct Answer: contract

18. Choose the correct sentence:

Correct Answer: I agree with you.

19. The synonym of “commence” is:

Correct Answer: begin

20. If it _____ tomorrow, we will stay at home.

Correct Answer: rains
